## Types of Cookies

Below you can find the different types of cookies we use on our site. Both first-party cookies (placed by the site you visit) and third-party cookies (placed by servers other than the site you visit) are used on our site.

### 1. Types of Cookies According to Duration of Use
Our website uses session cookies and persistent cookies according to their duration of use.
* **Session Cookie:** Used to ensure the continuity of the session, and these cookies are deleted when the user closes their browser.
* **Persistent Cookie:** These are not deleted when the internet browser is closed and are automatically deleted at a certain date or after a certain period.

### 2. First-Party and Third-Party Cookies
Whether a cookie is first-party or third-party depends on the cookie placed by the website or domain.
* **First-Party Cookies:** Placed directly by the website the user visits, i.e., the URL shown in the browser's address bar (`acrtech.com`).
* **Third-Party Cookies:** Placed by a domain other than the domain the user visits.

### 3. Types of Cookies According to Purpose of Use

* **Strictly Necessary Cookies:** These cookies are necessary for the operation of our website. These cookies are first-party cookies and process personal data during the session and are automatically deleted when the session ends. (e.g., logging in, filling out forms).
* **Performance and Analytics Cookies:** Thanks to these cookies, we can analyze your use of our site and the performance of our site to improve the services we provide to you.
* **Advertising/Marketing Cookies:** These cookies are placed by our advertising partners through our website and are third-party cookies. They are used to profile your interests and show you relevant advertisements.
* **Functional Cookies:** These types of cookies are used to make our website more functional and personalized (language preference, region selection, etc.).

## Purposes of Processing Personal Data and Legal Grounds

**Strictly Necessary Cookies:** Used to fulfill an information society service you have requested. Your personal data collected through these cookies is processed within the scope of Article 5, paragraph (2), subparagraph (c) of the Law "It is necessary to process the personal data of parties of a contract, provided that the processing is directly related to the execution or performance of the contract" or subparagraph (f) "Processing of data is necessary for the legitimate interests pursued by the data controller, provided that this processing shall not violate the fundamental rights and freedoms of the data subject".

## Rights of the Data Subject

Pursuant to the provisions of Article 11 of the Law, you have the following rights regarding your personal data processed via cookies:

* To learn whether personal data is processed or not,
* To request information if personal data has been processed,
* To learn the purpose of processing personal data and whether they are used appropriately for their purpose,
* To know the third parties to whom personal data is transferred domestically or abroad,
* To request correction of personal data in case of incomplete or incorrect processing,
* To request the deletion or destruction of personal data,
* To request notification of these operations to third parties to whom personal data has been transferred in case of correction, deletion, or destruction of personal data,
* To object to the occurrence of a result against the person himself by analyzing the processed data exclusively through automated systems,
* To request compensation for the damage in case of damage due to the unlawful processing of personal data.
