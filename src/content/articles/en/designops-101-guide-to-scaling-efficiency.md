## Growing the Value and Impact of Design: What is DesignOps?

In today's world where digital products and services are becoming more complex, design teams are not just "drawing screens" but assuming a strategic role. However, this growth brings operational burdens along with it. **DesignOps (Design Operations)** is the orchestration of people, processes, and craft in order to amplify design's value and impact. As acrtech, we establish the necessary infrastructure so that your designers can focus on designing, not bureaucracy.

## Why is DesignOps a Critical Need for Your Business?

As design teams grow and organizations understand the value of design, expectations from designers increase. DesignOps solves the following challenges to meet this increasing demand and produce scalable solutions:

* **Managing Growing Teams:** Scaling design teams and keeping evolving processes under control.
* **Finding the Right Talent:** Hiring and retaining people with the right skills.
* **Efficient Workflows:** Reducing bureaucracy that clogs creative processes and creating efficient workflows.
* **Increasing Output Quality:** Improving the quality and impact of design deliverables.

## acrtech's DesignOps Approach: 3 Main Components

With the DesignOps menu we created based on NN/g standards, we focus on three main areas for your business:

### 1. How We Work Together

We strengthen collaboration by organizing the structure, communication, and culture of teams.

* **Organization:** Clarifying team structure, role definitions, and the role of the design department.
* **Collaborate:** Establishing effective meeting rituals, communication environments, and communities of practice.
* **Humanize:** Making hiring, onboarding, and career development paths employee-centric.

### 2. How We Get Work Done

We increase efficiency by standardizing processes and harmonizing tools.

* **Standardize:** Mapping the design process, determining guiding principles, and using consistent toolsets.
* **Harmonize:** Creating a common language with Design Systems, research repositories, and asset management.
* **Prioritize:** Identifying bottlenecks in the workflow and accurately estimating projects according to capacity.

### 3. How Our Work Creates Impact

We make the value design adds to the business visible and measurable.

* **Measure:** Defining "done" definitions for design quality and tracking success metrics.
* **Socialize:** Sharing success stories and explaining the value of design within the organization.
* **Enable:** Preparing trainings and playbooks so that non-design teams can also understand design.

## DesignOps: A Role or a Mindset?

DesignOps is not just a new "Manager" title; it is an organizational culture change.

* **DesignOps Role:** In large teams, we can position dedicated people focusing on processes (DesignOps Managers, UX Producers).
* **DesignOps Mindset:** Every organization does not need to open a role immediately. As acrtech, we ensure your existing team gains a "DesignOps Mindset" focused on improving processes and increasing efficiency.
