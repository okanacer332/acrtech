# Fidanys Field and Nursery Management System

Fidanys is designed for nursery operations where stock, field movement, pallet identity, color, QR tracking, and sales data must stay connected.

## Problem

Field operations often carry their memory in notebooks, WhatsApp images, spreadsheets, and individual experience. When that information is scattered, stock accuracy, sales visibility, and profitability become fragile.

## Product Direction

Fidanys turns field work into a digital memory. Each plant group, pallet, color, movement, and transaction can become traceable. The product also adds inflation-adjusted real profit analysis so that visible revenue and actual gain can be read separately.

## Core Capabilities

- Nursery stock tracking
- Pallet and QR identity
- Field movement records
- Color and quality references
- Sales and collection tracking
- Inflation-adjusted real profit analysis
- Operational reporting

## ACRTECH Perspective

The value of Fidanys is not only digitization. It is awareness. When the field speaks through clean records, the business can see what it really earns, where stock moves, and which decisions need attention.
