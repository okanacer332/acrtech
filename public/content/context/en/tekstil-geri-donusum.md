# Textile Recycling Operation System

Textile recycling operations depend on material movement, classification, supplier records, stock accuracy, and sales flow. A digital system can make that movement visible from intake to output.

## Problem

When textile material is tracked manually, quality classes, weights, supplier history, and warehouse movement can become difficult to verify. This creates reporting gaps and profit uncertainty.

## Product Direction

The system can connect incoming material, sorting, quality classes, stock areas, production movement, sales, and reporting in one operational structure.

## Core Capabilities

- Supplier and intake records
- Material class and quality tracking
- Weight and stock movement
- Warehouse visibility
- Sales and dispatch records
- Reporting for cost, stock, and margin

## ACRTECH Perspective

Recycling software should respect the pace of the floor. Fast entry, clear labels, and reliable reports matter more than decorative complexity.
