# Dell Cloud Service Experience

Cloud service products need to make complex infrastructure understandable. The interface must show service health, cost, capacity, security, and support without turning the screen into noise.

## Problem

Enterprise customers often struggle to connect cloud usage with business impact. Technical metrics exist, but they may not be presented in a way that helps decision-makers act.

## Product Direction

The experience can bring infrastructure overview, resource usage, alerts, service tickets, and cost signals into a clean operational dashboard.

## Core Capabilities

- Service and resource overview
- Uptime and incident tracking
- Cost and usage summaries
- Security and compliance indicators
- Support ticket flow
- Role-based dashboard views

## ACRTECH Perspective

Cloud products work best when technical depth and executive clarity live together. Engineers need detail; managers need signal.
