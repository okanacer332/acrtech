# B2C Online Education Platform

The B2C online education model needs more than video hosting. It needs a complete learning journey: discovery, enrollment, progress, reminders, assessment, and long-term engagement.

## Problem

Many education platforms lose students after registration because the content, progress feedback, and motivation loops are disconnected. The product must keep learning visible and easy to resume.

## Product Direction

The platform can combine course catalogs, lesson pages, progress tracking, payment, certificates, and content management in one coherent experience.

## Core Capabilities

- Course catalog and filtering
- Video and document lesson pages
- Student dashboard
- Progress tracking and completion rules
- Payment and subscription flows
- Instructor/admin content management
- Certificate and assessment modules

## ACRTECH Perspective

The strongest education products make the next learning action obvious. Design, content structure, and data should support momentum without overwhelming the student.
